    \(I_C=\alpha I_E\) \(I_C=\beta I_B\) \(I_B=(1-\alpha)I_E=\frac{I_E}{\beta+1}\)
    \(I_E=(\beta+1)I_B\)
    \(\beta=\frac{\alpha}{1-\alpha}\)
    \(\alpha=\frac{\beta}{\beta+1}\)
