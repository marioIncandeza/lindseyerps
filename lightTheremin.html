    Light Theremin

    The theremin is an instrument that is played without physically touching the instrument. A voltage divider is implemented with a photoresistor and a 10KÎ© resistor. The voltage across the photoresistor is read by the analog input sensor on the resistor, and based on that input, the tone() function sends pulses of varying frequency through the piezo.
